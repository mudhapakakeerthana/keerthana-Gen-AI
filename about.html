Project Overview

What this platform is building and why it matters.

The Problem

Indian retail investors often jump across multiple apps and websites to compare stocks, mutual funds, ETFs, and indices. Many tools present raw numbers without explaining risk, volatility, cost structure, or drawdowns, which makes confident decision-making difficult.

The Solution

NiveshIQ provides a unified, explainable analytics layer across asset classes. Investors can screen, compare, and filter based on meaningful metrics while maintaining transparency. Every signal aims to show context â€” not just a score.

What This Demo Includes

  • Cross-asset screener (stocks, mutual funds, ETFs, indices)
  • Watchlist starring with dynamic filtering and sorting
  • Market context cards (index values and daily movement)
  • Risk Simulator (personalized filtering by tolerance + horizon)
  • Chatbot integration for guided exploration

Next Enhancements

  • Live NSE/BSE/AMFI data integration
  • Portfolio import and allocation analysis
  • Factor-based explainable scoring models
  • Risk alerts (drawdown, volatility spikes, diversification gaps)
